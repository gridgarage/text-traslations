Origins

The swimwear brand Shellysunwear (currently based in the US) has its origins in the city of Caracas and has been promoted since its inception by its creator and founder Marisela Barazarte.

Shellyswimwear is a leading beachwear brand in The Americas region, with extensive experience in the manufacture and marketing of swimwear and beachwear for women. We offer the best tailor-made swimwear service, and we guarantee your satisfaction and happiness when purchasing any of our products.
